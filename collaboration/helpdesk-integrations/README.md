## Create tickets from incidents

Once connected, any team member can create or link helpdesk tickets from incidents:

- **From the incidents table**: Select one or more incidents and create a ticket.
- **From the incident details page**: Open the incident and create or link a helpdesk ticket directly.

## Automate with Playbooks

Use [Playbooks](../../playbooks/introduction-to-playbooks.md) to automate ticket creation whenever an incident triggers. This removes the manual step and ensures every incident gets a corresponding ticket.

## FAQs

### Are these integrations bi-directional?

Zendesk and Freshdesk both have a Spike app available in their respective marketplaces. The app allows users on those platforms to check incidents and take action from their helpdesk side.

Supportpal is one-way only. Spike sends incident data to Supportpal, but there is no app and no sync back.

### Does Spike access data from my helpdesk platform?

Spike does not access or store any data from your helpdesk platform, including tickets or user information.
