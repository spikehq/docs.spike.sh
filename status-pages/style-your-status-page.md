# Style your status page

Visit [Themes](https://statuspage.spike.sh/themes) from the sidebar to access all styling options. Changes preview in real time before you publish.

## Header

Customize the header color, add a logo, and include links to other parts of your website for consistent navigation.

## Cover image and color

Customize the cover section with a background image or color. You can also hide the cover entirely for a minimal layout.

## Footer

Add custom links, social media icons, and copyright information to the footer.

## Components and historical uptime

For each component, choose whether to display historical uptime data. Showing uptime history adds transparency for visitors.

{% hint style="info" %}
Any non-operational status counts as downtime. Planned maintenance is excluded.
{% endhint %}
